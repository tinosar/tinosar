## About Me

Economist and Certified Public Accountant, I've worked across several areas over the years — credit analysis, financial management, teaching, investment projects, and more.

K-Id was born around 15 years ago as a simple spreadsheet where I tracked my expenses. Over time it evolved into a fairly complex system to manage my own finances and my family's, with VBA automations and a UI built with a UserForm.

My idea was always to share it and convert everything into proper code — to build a real app. This would be my life's legacy. 🙂

With the advent of AI, and with the help of my friend Claude, that became possible. And here we are — with **K-Id** *(key-idea)*.
